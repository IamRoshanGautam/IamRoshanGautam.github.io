My Story

Hello, I am Roshan Gautam, you can call me Rosh. I am originally from the land of the Himalayas - Nepal. I was born and brought up in Kathmandu - the capital city of Nepal. I am a seasoned QA Automation Engineer with a passion for bridging software engineering and product management. My journey in tech has evolved from ensuring quality through automation to understanding the broader impact of technology on user experience.

At Verisk , I have led teams in developing critical applications, implemented automated testing frameworks, and played a key role in cloud migration efforts for IoT projects. My approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of user needs.

Currently pursuing my MS in Management Information Systems at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, I'm expanding my knowledge in project management and business analytics to better align technology with business objectives.
